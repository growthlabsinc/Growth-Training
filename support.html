⏱️ Timer System

The heart of Growth: Method is our advanced timer system, designed to support various training methodologies.

Timer Modes

Mode Description Best For Features
Stopwatch Count up from zero Open-ended sessions, endurance training Pause/Resume, Lap tracking
Countdown Count down from set time Timed exercises, focused work sessions Custom duration, Alert on completion
Interval Alternating work/rest periods HIIT, Pomodoro, Circuit training Custom intervals, Round tracking

Keep track of your training sessions without constantly opening the app.

What are Live Activities?

Live Activities display real-time timer information on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island (iPhone 14 Pro and later). They update automatically and provide quick access to timer controls.

Lock Screen Display

See your timer progress, current round, and controls directly on the Lock Screen.

Dynamic Island

Compact and expanded views in the Dynamic Island for iPhone 14 Pro+ users.

Real-time Updates

Timer counts in real-time without draining battery using native iOS features.

Enabling Live Activities

🔧 Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Live Activities not appearing
  1. Check iOS Settings → Growth → Live Activities is enabled
  2. Ensure you're on iOS 16.1 or later
  3. Restart your iPhone if the setting was recently changed
  4. Check that Do Not Disturb isn't blocking Live Activities
Timer stops when app is in background

This usually happens due to iOS battery optimization:

  1. Go to Settings → Growth → Background App Refresh → Enable
  2. Check Settings → Battery → ensure Growth isn't in Low Power Mode restrictions
  3. The timer should continue even in background with Live Activities enabled
Notifications not working
  1. Check Settings → Notifications → Growth → Allow Notifications is on
  2. Ensure notification settings in the app match your iOS settings
  3. Check Focus modes aren't blocking Growth notifications
  4. Try toggling notifications off and on in both iOS Settings and the app
AI Coach not responding

If the AI Coach isn't working properly:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Ensure you're signed in to your account
  3. Try force-closing and reopening the app
  4. Check Settings → AI Coach → ensure it's enabled
  5. Contact support if the issue persists
Data not syncing across devices
  1. Ensure you're logged in with the same account on all devices
  2. Check internet connectivity on both devices
  3. Go to Settings → Account → tap "Sync Now"
  4. Allow up to 5 minutes for sync to complete
  5. Try logging out and back in if sync issues persist

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Growth: Method different from other timer apps?

Growth: Method is specifically designed for personal development and training methodologies. Unlike generic timer apps, we offer:

  • Specialized timer modes for various training methods
  • AI-powered coaching and personalized recommendations
  • Comprehensive progress tracking and analytics
  • Live Activities and Dynamic Island support
  • Educational resources and structured programs
Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Core features work offline including:

  • All timer functions
  • Saved routines and sessions
  • Local progress tracking
  • Downloaded educational content

You'll need internet for: AI Coach, data sync, and downloading new content.

How accurate is the timer?

Our timer system is highly accurate, using iOS's native timing mechanisms. The timer continues running accurately even when the app is in the background or the phone is locked. Live Activities update in real-time without affecting battery life significantly.

Can I export my data?

Premium users can export their data in multiple formats:

  • CSV for spreadsheet analysis
  • JSON for developer use
  • PDF reports for records

Go to Settings → Privacy → Export Data to download your information.

Is my data backed up?

Yes, if you're signed in with an account, your data is automatically backed up to our secure cloud servers. You can also enable iCloud backup for additional redundancy. Local-only users should regularly export their data for backup.
